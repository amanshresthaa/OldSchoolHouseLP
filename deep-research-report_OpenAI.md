### Drinks offer direction that *is* explicitly stated

- A move toward a **premium draught range** and a **regularly rotating guest ale**. (Business Plan – Old School House v2, p.7)  
- Introduction of premium spirits “including current trends such as **artisanal gins and single malt whiskies**.” (Business Plan – Old School House v2, p.7)

### Hot drinks offer direction that *is* explicitly stated
The business plan states the pub will have:
- “an excellent hot drinks range,” supported by a **new coffee and artisanal tea offer**, **good coffee machine**, and **loyalty card**. (Business Plan – Old School House v2, p.7)

### Blueprint requirements for how the Menu page should be built
The website blueprint requires the Menu page to be a **real webpage (live HTML text)**, not a PDF-only menu (though PDF can be optional). It specifies logical sections (examples listed: food, drinks, desserts, Sunday roast, kids, brunch, specials), visible pricing without zooming, dietary markers and allergy note, and prominent CTAs. (Restaurant/Pub Website Blueprint, pp.2, 8)
